Coraline

Coraline is about a girl who moves into an old house, there she feels bored and ignored by her parents.
She later finds a secret door which led to another world where she has caring parents.
However, something is different from them, they had buttons for eyes. The "other mother" tries to convince her to stay in the world forever, making her trapped.
